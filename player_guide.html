Starter RPG

Character Sheet Player Guide

Core Rules

Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency vs. DC. DCs: 10 easy, 13 moderate, 16 hard, 19 very hard, 22 nearly impossible.

Advantage/Disadvantage: Roll 2d20, take higher/lower.

Abilities

  • Strength (STR): Physical power, lifting, melee damage.
  • Dexterity (DEX): Agility, stealth, ranged accuracy.
  • Constitution (CON): Endurance, resilience, hit points.
  • Intelligence (INT): Knowledge, investigation, spellcasting (Wizards).
  • Wisdom (WIS): Perception, insight, survival, spellcasting (Clerics/Druids).
  • Charisma (CHA): Persuasion, performance, spellcasting (Bards/Sorcerers/Warlocks).

Skills

  • Acrobatics (Dexterity)
  • Animal Handling (Wisdom)
  • Arcana (Intelligence)
  • Athletics (Strength)
  • Deception (Charisma)
  • History (Intelligence)
  • Insight (Wisdom)
  • Intimidation (Charisma)
  • Investigation (Intelligence)
  • Medicine (Wisdom)
  • Nature (Intelligence)
  • Perception (Wisdom)
  • Performance (Charisma)
  • Persuasion (Charisma)
  • Religion (Intelligence)
  • Sleight of Hand (Dexterity)
  • Stealth (Dexterity)
  • Survival (Wisdom)

Common Items & Gear

  • Weapons: Longsword (1d8 slashing), Shortbow (1d6 piercing), Dagger (1d4 piercing), Greatsword (2d6 slashing)
  • Armor: Leather Armor (AC 11 + DEX), Chain Mail (AC 16, STR 13), Plate Armor (AC 18, STR 15)
  • Shield: +2 AC
  • Consumables: Health Potion (heals 2d4+2 HP), Antitoxin (removes poison), Alchemist's Fire (1d4 fire/round)
  • Tools & Gear: Backpack, Rope (50 ft), Crowbar, Torch, Healer's Kit

Basic Spells

  • Fireball (Evocation, L3): Explosion of flame at range.
  • Cure Wounds (Evocation, L1): Heals target for 1d8 + modifier.
  • Mage Armor (Abjuration, L1): Magical force grants AC protection.
  • Magic Missile (Evocation, L1): Creates three darts of magical force.
  • Shield (Abjuration, L1): Invisible barrier boosts AC.
  • Bless (Enchantment, L1): Boosts allies' rolls.
  • Detect Magic (Divination, L1): Sense magic nearby.
  • Healing Word (Evocation, L1): Heals target at range.
  • Thunderwave (Evocation, L1): Thunderous force sweeps out.
  • Sleep (Enchantment, L1): Puts creatures to sleep.
  • Burning Hands (Evocation, L1): Sheet of flame from hands.

Combat

Roll initiative (d20 + AGI). On your turn: move + action (+ bonus action). One reaction per round.

Resting

Short Rest: 1 hr; spend hit dice to heal. Long Rest: 8 hr; restore HP/resources.

Leveling

Proficiency Bonus +2 at L1; increases at 5, 9, 13.
